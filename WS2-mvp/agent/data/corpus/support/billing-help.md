# JioFiber Billing and Payments Help

## Payment Methods

### What payment methods does Jio accept for JioFiber?
Jio accepts a wide range of payment methods:
- **UPI**: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay UPI, or any UPI app
- **Credit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay
- **Debit Cards**: All major bank debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- **Net Banking**: All major Indian banks
- **Jio Wallet / JioPay**: Balance in your Jio payments wallet
- **Amazon Pay Balance**: Via Amazon app or website
- **Cash**: At Jio Stores, Jio Point retailers, and authorized partners
- **Cheque/DD**: Not accepted for JioFiber recharges

### Can I pay via the MyJio app?
Yes. The MyJio app is the primary payment channel. Open the app, go to JioFiber, select your plan or outstanding amount, and choose your preferred payment method. Payments are reflected instantly.

### Can I pay at a Jio Store?
Yes. Visit any Jio Store or authorized Jio Point retailer. Provide your JioFiber registered mobile number or account ID. You can pay by cash, card, or UPI at the store.

### Is there a convenience fee for online payments?
No. Jio does not charge any convenience or transaction fee for online recharges or bill payments through MyJio, jio.com, or partner platforms.

## Autopay

### How do I set up autopay?
1. Open the MyJio app
2. Navigate to JioFiber section
3. Tap on "Autopay" or "Manage Payments"
4. Choose your payment method - UPI mandate, credit card, or debit card
5. Set the maximum amount you authorize per transaction
6. Confirm the mandate with your bank (UPI PIN or OTP verification)

### When does autopay charge me?
Autopay triggers 1-3 days before your plan expiry date. This ensures uninterrupted service. You will receive an SMS notification before and after the auto-debit.

### Can I cancel autopay?
Yes. Go to MyJio app > JioFiber > Autopay > Manage > Cancel. You can also cancel the UPI mandate directly from your bank's UPI app. After cancellation, you will need to recharge manually.

### What if autopay fails?
If the auto-debit fails (insufficient balance, bank issue, expired card), you will receive an SMS notification. Jio will retry once. If it fails again, you will need to recharge manually before your plan expires to avoid service interruption.

### Can I change my autopay amount or method?
Yes. Cancel the existing autopay mandate and set up a new one with the updated payment method or amount. Go to MyJio > JioFiber > Autopay to manage.

## Bill Cycle

### What is my JioFiber billing cycle?
JioFiber uses a prepaid billing model. Your plan starts on the day of recharge and runs for 30 days (monthly plan), 90 days (quarterly), or 365 days (annual). There is no fixed monthly billing date - it depends on when you recharge.

### How do I check my plan expiry date?
Open MyJio app > JioFiber. Your current plan, start date, and expiry date are displayed on the dashboard. You also receive SMS reminders 3 days and 1 day before expiry.

### What happens if I recharge before my current plan expires?
If you recharge with the same plan, the validity is added to your remaining days. If you upgrade to a higher plan, the new plan activates immediately and the remaining days of the old plan are adjusted. If you downgrade, the new plan takes effect after the current plan expires.

### Can I get a monthly invoice?
Yes. Jio provides a monthly statement/invoice accessible through:
- MyJio app > JioFiber > Bills & Statements
- jio.com > Sign in > My Account > Statements
- Email - if you have opted for email billing

## Late Payment and Grace Period

### What happens if I do not recharge on time?
After your plan expires, your service will be paused. You will not be able to use the internet or make landline calls. You typically get a grace period of approximately 15 days during which you can recharge and resume service without losing your connection.

### Will I lose my JioFiber number or connection if I miss a payment?
Not immediately. During the grace period (approximately 15 days after expiry), you can recharge and your connection resumes. After the grace period, your connection may be deactivated and the number released. Extended non-payment (60-90 days) may result in permanent disconnection and equipment recovery.

### Is there a late payment fee?
No. JioFiber operates on a prepaid model, so there is no late fee or penalty. The service simply stops when the plan expires. Recharge at any time during the grace period to resume.

### Can I get a temporary extension if I cannot pay immediately?
Jio does not offer formal bill extensions for home broadband. However, the grace period effectively serves as a buffer. In some cases, contacting support at 1800-889-9999 and explaining the situation may result in a short courtesy extension.

## Billing Disputes and Refunds

### I was charged twice for the same recharge. What do I do?
1. Check your bank statement to confirm the double charge
2. Open MyJio app > Help > Raise a Complaint > Billing
3. Provide the transaction IDs and amount details
4. Jio will investigate and process a refund within 7-10 business days if the double charge is confirmed
5. Alternatively, call 1800-889-9999 with transaction details

### I recharged but my plan was not activated. What do I do?
1. Wait 15-30 minutes - sometimes activation takes time
2. Check your payment status in MyJio > JioFiber > Transaction History
3. If the payment shows as successful but the plan is not active, raise a complaint in MyJio app
4. If the payment failed but money was deducted, it will be auto-refunded to your account within 5-7 business days
5. Contact 1800-889-9999 if the issue is not resolved within 24 hours

### Can I get a refund if I cancel mid-cycle?
Jio's policy on mid-cycle refunds varies. For monthly plans, refunds are generally not provided for unused days. For annual or semi-annual plans cancelled early, a pro-rata refund may be considered. Contact Jio support to discuss your specific case.

### I see an unauthorized charge on my account. What should I do?
1. Check if it is an autopay debit you may have forgotten about
2. Check if another family member recharged the account
3. If the charge is genuinely unauthorized, call 1800-889-9999 immediately
4. Also raise a dispute with your bank for the transaction
5. Change your MyJio account password and disable autopay as a precaution

## GST and Tax

### Is GST included in the JioFiber plan price?
Yes. All JioFiber plan prices displayed on jio.com and MyJio are inclusive of 18% GST. The amount you see is the amount you pay. No additional tax is added at checkout.

### Can I get a GST invoice for my JioFiber payment?
Yes. GST invoices are available for all JioFiber payments:
- Go to jio.com > Sign in > My Account > Tax Invoice
- Or MyJio app > JioFiber > Bills & Statements > Download Invoice
- The invoice includes Jio's GSTIN, your service details, taxable amount, CGST, SGST/IGST breakup, and total amount

### Can I claim input tax credit (ITC) on JioFiber for my business?
If you are a registered GST taxpayer and use JioFiber for business purposes, you may be eligible to claim ITC on the GST component. Ensure the JioFiber connection is in your business name or linked to your GSTIN. Download the GST invoice from jio.com for your records.

### What is Jio's GSTIN?
Jio's GSTIN varies by state (circle). It is printed on each GST invoice. For Maharashtra (Mumbai), it is typically 27AABCR1718E1ZM. Verify from your actual invoice for accuracy.

## Mid-Cycle Plan Changes

### Can I upgrade my plan in the middle of the billing cycle?
Yes. You can upgrade anytime through MyJio app. The upgrade takes effect immediately. You will be charged the difference between your current plan and the new plan, prorated for the remaining days.

### Can I downgrade my plan mid-cycle?
Downgrades are scheduled for the next billing cycle. Your current plan continues until it expires, and the lower plan takes effect upon your next recharge.

### If I upgrade, do I lose my remaining data or benefits?
No. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you get the benefits of the higher plan for the remaining days plus any additional data or OTT access that comes with it. Your unused benefits from the current plan carry forward within the same cycle.

### How is the prorated amount calculated for upgrades?
The prorated charge is calculated as: (New plan daily rate - Old plan daily rate) x Remaining days in current cycle. For example, upgrading from Rs 699 to Rs 999 plan with 15 days remaining: (Rs 33.3 - Rs 23.3) x 15 = Rs 150 approximately.
