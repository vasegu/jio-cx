# Jio Home Broadband - General FAQ

## Account Management

### Q1: How do I create a Jio account?
Download the MyJio app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Register using your Jio mobile number. You will receive an OTP for verification. Once verified, your account is active and linked to all Jio services.

### Q2: How do I check my JioFiber account details?
Open the MyJio app and tap on the JioFiber section. You can view your plan details, data usage, billing cycle, and account ID. Alternatively, log in at jio.com with your registered mobile number.

### Q3: Can I link multiple Jio services to one account?
Yes. Your Jio ID (registered mobile number) can be linked to JioFiber, Jio mobile, JioTV, JioCloud, and other Jio services. All appear under a single MyJio dashboard.

### Q4: How do I update my registered mobile number?
Visit your nearest Jio Store with a valid government-issued photo ID. A store executive will help you update the registered mobile number after identity verification. This cannot be done online for security reasons.

### Q5: How do I update my email address on my Jio account?
Open MyJio app > Menu > My Account > Edit Profile. You can update your email address directly. A verification email will be sent to the new address.

### Q6: Can someone else manage my JioFiber account?
Yes. You can add an authorized representative by visiting a Jio Store with both parties present along with valid photo IDs. The authorized person can then manage recharges and basic account operations.

### Q7: How do I deactivate or close my JioFiber connection?
Call 1800-889-9999 or visit a Jio Store. You will need to return the ONT (router) device. Any pending bills must be cleared. Security deposit (if applicable) will be refunded within 15-30 business days after equipment return.

### Q8: What documents are needed for a new JioFiber connection?
You need a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID) and address proof matching the installation address. Aadhaar-based eKYC is the fastest method.

## Recharge and Plans

### Q9: What JioFiber plans are available?
JioFiber offers plans starting from Rs 399/month (30 Mbps) going up to Rs 8,499/month (1 Gbps). Plans include unlimited data with a fair usage policy, free voice calling, and OTT app subscriptions depending on the tier. Check jio.com/fiber for the latest plan lineup.

### Q10: How do I recharge my JioFiber?
You can recharge through the MyJio app, jio.com, or any authorized Jio retailer. Online payment methods include UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and Jio wallet.

### Q11: Can I recharge JioFiber using someone else's phone?
Yes. Anyone can recharge your JioFiber account if they have your JioFiber ID or registered mobile number. They can use the MyJio app, jio.com, or visit a retailer.

### Q12: What happens when my JioFiber plan expires?
Your internet service will stop after the plan expiry date. You will have a grace period of approximately 15 days during which you can recharge and resume service. After the grace period, your connection may be deactivated.

### Q13: Can I change my JioFiber plan mid-cycle?
Yes. You can upgrade your plan anytime through MyJio app. The upgrade takes effect immediately and you pay the prorated difference. Downgrades typically take effect from the next billing cycle.

### Q14: Is there a lock-in period for JioFiber plans?
Monthly plans have no lock-in. Some semi-annual and annual plans may offer discounts in exchange for a commitment period. Check plan details before subscribing.

### Q15: Do JioFiber plans include landline calling?
Yes. All JioFiber plans include unlimited free voice calling to any network across India via the provided landline connection. International calling is available at standard Jio rates.

## Payments

### Q16: What payment methods are accepted for JioFiber?
Accepted methods include UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), debit cards, credit cards, net banking, Amazon Pay, Jio wallet, and cash at Jio Stores or authorized retailers.

### Q17: Is autopay available for JioFiber?
Yes. You can set up autopay through MyJio app using UPI, credit card, or debit card. Go to MyJio > JioFiber > Autopay > Set Up. You will be charged automatically before your plan expires.

### Q18: Will I get a payment receipt?
Yes. A digital receipt is sent to your registered email and is also available in the MyJio app under the billing section. GST invoices can be downloaded from jio.com.

## OTT and Entertainment

### Q19: Which OTT apps are included with JioFiber?
Depending on your plan, you may get access to Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar, SonyLIV, ZEE5, JioCinema, Voot, and more. Higher-tier plans include more OTT subscriptions. Check your specific plan details on MyJio.

### Q20: How do I activate my OTT subscriptions?
OTT apps are auto-activated when you recharge with an eligible plan. Open the MyJio app, go to the OTT section, and you will see activation links for each included app. Sign in using your Jio credentials.

### Q21: Can I use OTT subscriptions on multiple devices?
OTT access provided through JioFiber is linked to your Jio account. The number of simultaneous streams depends on each OTT platform's own policy. Typically 1-2 screens are supported per app.

### Q22: My OTT subscription is not showing as active. What do I do?
Ensure your JioFiber plan includes OTT. Then, in MyJio app, go to the OTT section and tap "Activate" next to the app. If it still does not work, log out of the OTT app and log back in with your Jio credentials. If the issue persists, contact support at 1800-889-9999.

## MyJio App

### Q23: What can I do with the MyJio app?
The MyJio app lets you recharge, check usage, view bills, manage autopay, activate OTT apps, raise complaints, chat with support, check plan details, and manage your Jio account across all services.

### Q24: The MyJio app is not loading. What should I do?
Ensure you have the latest version from Play Store or App Store. Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > MyJio > Clear Cache). Check your internet connection. If the issue continues, uninstall and reinstall the app.

### Q25: Can I track my JioFiber installation request on MyJio?
Yes. After placing an installation request, you can track it in the MyJio app under JioFiber > Installation Status. You will also receive SMS updates on your registered number.

## Referrals and Rewards

### Q26: Does Jio have a referral program for JioFiber?
Yes. Existing JioFiber customers can refer friends and family through the MyJio app. Both the referrer and the new customer can earn benefits such as cashback or extra data. Check MyJio for current referral offers.

### Q27: How do I refer someone for JioFiber?
Open MyJio app > Refer & Earn > Share your referral code or link via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media. When the referred person activates a JioFiber connection using your code, both parties earn rewards.

### Q28: What rewards do I get for referrals?
Rewards vary by ongoing promotions. Typical rewards include Rs 500-2000 cashback, extra data, or plan discounts. Check the latest offer on the MyJio app.

## General Service

### Q29: What is the minimum contract period for JioFiber?
Monthly plans have no minimum contract. You can cancel anytime. Equipment must be returned upon cancellation.

### Q30: Can I shift my JioFiber connection to a new address?
Yes. Raise a relocation request through MyJio app or call 1800-889-9999. Relocation is subject to JioFiber availability at the new address. A technician visit will be scheduled for reinstallation.

### Q31: Is JioFiber available in my area?
Check availability by entering your address or PIN code on jio.com/fiber or in the MyJio app. JioFiber is available across 150+ cities in India, including most major metros and tier-2 cities.

### Q32: What is the JioFiber security deposit?
A refundable security deposit of Rs 2,500 is charged for the ONT (router) device provided at installation. This is refunded upon returning the device in working condition when you close the connection.

### Q33: Can I use my own router with JioFiber?
The Jio ONT device is required for the fiber connection. However, you can connect your own router to the Jio ONT via Ethernet for additional coverage or features. Jio support only covers the provided ONT device.

### Q34: Does JioFiber support IPv6?
JioFiber has been rolling out IPv6 support. Availability depends on your area and equipment. Most connections support dual-stack IPv4 and IPv6.
