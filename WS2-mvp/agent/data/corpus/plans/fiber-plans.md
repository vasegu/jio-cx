# JioFiber Broadband Plans

All JioFiber plans include unlimited data (no daily cap), a Wi-Fi router at no extra cost, symmetric upload/download speeds, and free voice calling via the Jio Home Phone (landline) service.

## Plan Overview

| Plan | Monthly Price (INR) | Speed | OTT Apps Included |
|------|--------------------:|------:|-------------------|
| Silver | 599 | 30 Mbps | JioTV+, JioCinema (free tier) |
| Gold | 999 | 100 Mbps | JioTV+, JioCinema Premium, 14 OTT apps |
| Diamond | 1,499 | 150 Mbps | JioTV+, JioCinema Premium, Netflix Basic, 16 OTT apps |
| Platinum | 2,499 | 300 Mbps | JioTV+, JioCinema Premium, Netflix Standard, Amazon Prime, 17 OTT apps |
| Titanium | 3,999 | 1 Gbps | JioTV+, JioCinema Premium, Netflix Premium, Amazon Prime, 17+ OTT apps |

## Common Features Across All Plans

- **No daily data cap** - truly unlimited usage
- **Symmetric speeds** - same upload and download speed
- **Free installation** - standard installation at no charge (with router)
- **Free router** - dual-band Wi-Fi router provided and maintained by Jio
- **Free voice calling** - unlimited local + STD calls via Jio landline
- **JioTV+** - 550+ live TV channels included on all plans
- **No lock-in period** - month-to-month billing, cancel anytime
- **Jio Set Top Box** - available for INR 1,999 one-time (optional, for TV streaming via HDMI)

## Semi-Annual and Annual Discounts

Jio offers discounts for longer commitment periods:
- **3-month plans** - small discount vs monthly
- **6-month plans** - approximately 1 month free
- **12-month plans** - approximately 2 months free

Annual plans offer the best value. Exact pricing varies and should be confirmed on jio.com or via the MyJio app.

## Availability

JioFiber is available in 150+ cities across India. Coverage depends on whether fiber infrastructure has been laid in the customer's area. Customers can check availability by entering their address on jio.com or the MyJio app.
