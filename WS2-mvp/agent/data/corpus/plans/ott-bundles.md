## Key OTT Service Descriptions

### JioTV+
- 550+ live TV channels across news, sports, entertainment, regional, kids
- Available on all plans
- Supports multiple languages
- Includes catch-up TV (watch shows aired in last 7 days)
- No additional app download needed if using Jio STB or AirFiber device

### JioCinema
- **Free tier (Silver):** Ad-supported, limited content library, SD/HD
- **Premium (Gold and above):** Ad-free experience, full content library including originals, Hollywood movies, HBO content, 4K streaming
- JioCinema carries HBO Originals, Peacock content, and other international titles

### Netflix
- Separate Netflix app/account, activated via Jio partnership
- Tier depends on broadband plan (Basic/Standard/Premium)
- Full Netflix library including originals, movies, series
- Can be used on any device (not limited to Jio devices)

### Amazon Prime
- Included from Platinum plan onwards
- Full Amazon Prime membership: Prime Video, Prime Music, Prime Reading, free/faster delivery on Amazon.in
- Separate Amazon account linked via Jio

### Disney+ Hotstar
- Included from Diamond plan onwards
- Covers Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, National Geographic content
- Live sports including IPL, ICC cricket, football leagues
- Hotstar Originals and regional content

### Sony LIV
- Available from Gold plan onwards
- Sony entertainment content, sports (WWE, UFC, UEFA), originals
- Premium tier on higher plans

### Zee5
- Available from Gold plan onwards
- Zee entertainment network content, originals, movies
- Multi-language content (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, etc.)

## OTT Access Methods

1. **MyJio App** - Activate and manage OTT subscriptions from the MyJio app
2. **Jio Set Top Box** - All OTT apps pre-installed, navigate via remote
3. **JioAirFiber Device** - HDMI output with built-in OTT apps
4. **Individual Apps** - Download each OTT app on phone/tablet/smart TV, login with linked credentials
5. **Web Browsers** - Access most OTT services via their websites

## Important Notes

- OTT subscriptions are tied to the active broadband plan; they deactivate if the plan expires or is downgraded
- Upgrading the plan immediately unlocks the higher-tier OTT bundle
- Downgrading the plan removes access to OTT services not included in the lower tier
- Some OTT apps may require separate account creation (e.g., Netflix, Amazon) which is then linked to the Jio subscription
- OTT content and bundled apps may change based on Jio's partnerships; always verify current offerings on jio.com or MyJio app
